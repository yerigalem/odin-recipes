Pizza

Home Description

A famous Italina dish, made with dough, cheese, tomato paste and pepperoni slices.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Flour
  • Sour Dough
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Paste
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Mix water and flour
  2. Add sour dough
  3. Prepare dough in a circle
  4. Spread tomato paste on top
  5. Sprinkle cheese
  6. Cut pepperoni and add on the dough
  7. Bake pizza
  8. Take out of oven after around 20 minutes
