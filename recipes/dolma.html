Description

Well-known Armenian dish with meat stuffed inside grape leaves.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Rice
  • Tomato Paste
  • Fresh Grape Leaves

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef with rice and tomato paste
  2. Put mix inside each leaf and roll leaf
  3. Boil for 30 minutes
