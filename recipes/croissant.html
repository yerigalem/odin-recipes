Description

The most famous French pastry, a plain piece of pastry made with butter and dough.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Water
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Mix water and flour into dough
  2. Spread dough
  3. Add butter
  4. Twirl finished dough into shape
  5. Bake for forty minutes
